How to Start a Blog in Rwanda: Step-by-Step Guide

Starting a blog in Rwanda is easier than you think. Follow these simple steps to launch your first blog and share your ideas with the world.

Step 1: Choose Your Niche

Decide what your blog will be about. Popular niches: tech, blogging, finance, lifestyle.

Step 2: Select Free or Cheap Hosting

For beginners, free hosting is enough. Later you can upgrade. Examples: GitHub Pages (free), Hostinger (cheap).

Step 3: Register Your Domain

Pick a simple, memorable name. Example: techstephub.com

Step 4: Install a Website Platform

Use WordPress, Blogger, or GitHub Pages. For your site, we are using GitHub Pages (static HTML site).

Step 5: Write Your First Blog Post

Start with an article that solves a problem or shares valuable knowledge. Include images and clear headings.

Step 6: Promote Your Blog

Share on social media, WhatsApp, and Facebook groups. Consistent promotion brings traffic.

Optional: Monetize Your Blog

Once traffic grows, add Google AdSense or affiliate links to earn money from your blog.
