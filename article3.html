How to Make Money Online with a Website

A website can help you earn money if you use the right methods.

Method 1: Google AdSense

Google AdSense shows ads on your website and pays you when people click.

Method 2: Affiliate Marketing

You earn commission by promoting products or services.

Method 3: Selling Your Own Services

You can sell skills like design, writing, or consulting.

Be Patient

Making money online takes time and consistency.
